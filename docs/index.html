What it is

A protocol, not a prompt An explicit, auditable procedure for the moments that decide a quit: the 2 a.m. craving, the pack already in hand, the slip, the silence. Every claim traced to published research; the provenance of every rule labeled.
A witness with long memory The sponsor keeps your logbook (encrypted at rest), knows your contract and your risk map, and is on call at the hours human support is not. Zero shame: slips are data, nothing banked is ever lost.
Open end to end The protocol, the bot, the ethics charter that binds the operators, the safety rules, and the evaluation are all public and MIT-licensed.

The honest part

  • Not a medical device, not an emergency service. In a crisis, call your local emergency number or findahelpline.com — never wait on a bot.
  • Not a panacea. Professional support plus medication holds the strongest evidence. If you have access to it, take it; the sponsor works alongside, as the continuity layer.
  • Strict scope. Tobacco and cannabis co-use only. Alcohol and opioid dependence get one answer: a clinician — on those paths, a slip can kill.
  • No outcome promises. Most quit attempts fail; evidence-based support improves the odds, and we publish the evidence.
